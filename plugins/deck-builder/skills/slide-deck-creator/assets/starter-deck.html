How we work

  • Default to open. Code, decisions, and documents are shared by design.
  • Write things down. P2 is our memory; meetings are not.
  • Trust the team. Hire carefully, then get out of the way.
  • Optimise for makers. Calendars are not a status symbol.
  • Ship in small slices. A weekly release beats a quarterly one.
  • Care about craft. Polish is a feature.
